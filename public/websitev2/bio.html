ABOUT

Hi! I'm George, and I'm an MA Graphic Design student studying at the University of Portsmouth, but I'm originally from Canterbury, Kent. This website was painfully lovingly made from scratch by me :)



My main interests currently anime & manga and games, in particular the Fire Emblem series and One Piece.

Design-wise, I like to make mock ups of merchandise designs and illustrations for my interests as I think that official ones are often lacking visually and I want to create something different and exciting for fellow fans. My style mostly uses clean lines and shapes, pastel & muted colours and simple layouts.

I use Procreate for my illustration work and to create assets for my designs. I aim to learn Adobe Software over the next year as they are most-used in the industry
